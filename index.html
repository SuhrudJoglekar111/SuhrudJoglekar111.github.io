About Me

I am a mechanical engineer with a passion for robotic systems. Through my education and experience, I have honed my skills in designing, developing and executing innovative projects that have the potential to aid in the well-being of the general public.

My interest in robotics started early on when I participated in college-level competitions under the guidance of my professors. The creative aspect of this field always attracted me, although working on the projects escalated my curiosity to pursue a master's degree in Robotics and Autonomous Systems to further deepen my knowledge and skills in the field. Combining my experience from working on projects during both degrees, I found myself strengthening my technical skills such as designing, building, and testing robotic systems. Although, I am always eager to further sharpen those skills.
