The Kharcha App: An Expense Tracking System

What:

The Kharcha app is an intuitive tool for managing shared expenses within groups. It simplifies expense tracking and ensures equitable distribution of expenses among participants.

How:

The app features:

  • Expense Management: Tracks and categorizes expenses to provide clear financial insights.
  • Equitable Distribution: Ensures fair sharing of expenses among group members, enhancing transparency and accountability.

The development process involved:

  • Design: User-centric design to create an intuitive interface for seamless expense management.
  • Development: Implementation of robust backend systems for secure and reliable data handling.
  • Testing: Validation through real-world usage scenarios to ensure functionality and user satisfaction.

Results:

The Kharcha app offers:

  • Expense Tracking: Provides comprehensive tracking of shared expenses, enhancing financial management.
  • User Accessibility: Ensures ease of use and accessibility for managing expenses within groups.

The project simplifies expense management and promotes financial transparency among users, facilitating efficient expense tracking and management.
